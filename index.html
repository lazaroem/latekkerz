LA Tekkerz

LA Tekkerz came to be because of the passion we have for soccer, and from the inspiration we get when helping players of all ages elevate their game. Based in the San Fernando Valley and greater Los Angeles area, we help athletes pursue their goals and push themselves to newer heights.



We focus on small group and individual training sessions that center mainly on technique and speed/agility. Our sessions consist of game like scenarios, mental and physical reaction time, strength and conditioning, and as our name suggests, technique (aka Tekkerz). If you are commited to becoming a better player, becoming stronger and faster, and if you're ready to perfect your game, book a session with us now and let's begin training.

More About Us

Futbol

We specialize in all kinds of drills and exercises, offensive and defensive. Technique, footwork, body movement, shooting, mental and physical speed are all important apects of the game we focus on improving and elevating to the next level.

Speed and Agility

After ball mastery and technique, we believe strength and conditioning along with speed and agility are essential to becoming an impact player. Our speed and agility drills are done with and without the ball and focus on change of direction, balance, strength, and accelaration.

Private Sessions

Private 1-on-1 sessions are what we specialize in. We allow sessions up to groups of 5, where larger groups are considered specialized group sessions, and gorups larger than 8 are classified as clinics. Our private sessions focus on individual growth and improvement, group sessions are a mix of team exercises and individual work, and clinincs usually focus on ball mastery ball work/dribbling.
