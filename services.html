Private 1-on-1 Training

Best Training in the Valley

Are you not getting enough passes or technique at your practice sessions? Are you a beginner trying to learn the game? Are you a premier player, semi-pro or professional looking to learn new things or get extra practice in?


If this sounds like you, you are in the right place. Wether you want to get an extra practice session per week, or you want to focus on a specific skill or weakness, we specialize in improving your game.


With 1-on-1 sessions, you get to receieve personal feedback and full attention to your skills and growth. We get to build personalized plans and training sessions for you that are catered to rapidly improving and sharpening your skills. The two main areas we focus on are technique and speed & agility but are not limited to:


General technique
Passing/Touches/First-Touch
Awareness
Shooting/Weak Foot/Crossing/Attacking
Tackling/Defending/Positional Stance & Sense
Technique/Dribbling/Foot Mastery/Offensive Moves
Upper/Lower Body Strength
Speed & Agility/Body Movement/Fitness/Speed/Quick Feet
Brain Training/Reaction Timing
Social Skills/Confidence/Team Work


If you would like to work on any of the above, or if you would just like to try train in general, don't wait. Book a session with us and let's begin working.

If you aren't comfortable with a 1 on 1, you can bring a friend/s. The session will then become a small group session in which we can do a load of different drills.

Position/Skill Specific Training

Training for All Ages and Levels

Clients tend to love position specific training when coming to us. It makes sense for a defender to want to improve their defending skills and us to focus on their tackles, positioning, backtracking, and interceptions. Likewise for offensive players. It would not make much sense to force a defender to solely focus on shooting drills.


Positions we work on are:


Strikers
Wingers
Midfielders
Center Backs/Last Man
Full Backs
Goalkeeper (Program currently developing)


We also work with kids and adults of any age, and of any level.


Please let us know if you would like to work on a specific position or area, and we will gladly set up a practice plan for you.

Speed & Agility

Elevate your game with our lower body, speed, and body movement drills. Explosiveness is a key aspect to unleashing true potential, and we believe mixing in strength and conditioning with football is crucial for player development.

Technique, Dribbling, & Passing

At the core of football is technique, passing, and dribbling. These are essentials for any football player looking to play at the high level, and we have load of drills just for this. Whether it's short/long passes, tight space dribbling, ball control, or how to dribble past your opponent, we'll help you master these skills.
